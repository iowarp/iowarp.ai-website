Platform Overview

Four integrated engines bridge AI agents with scientific infrastructure.

CAE — The Way In

Content Assimilation Engine ingests 50+ scientific formats (HDF5, NetCDF, ROOT), normalizes, enriches semantics, and delivers AI-ready context.

CTE — The Mover

Content Transfer Engine orchestrates ML-based predictive data movement across GPU HBM, NVMe, parallel FS, and tape.

CEI — The Finder

Content Exploration Interface (WarpGPT) enables natural language queries at petabyte scale.

PPI — The Connector

Platform Plugins Interface integrates HPC schedulers (Slurm, PBS), storage (Lustre, GPFS, S3), and instruments.
