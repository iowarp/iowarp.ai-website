Multi-Modality

Integrating diverse data sources into unified context

Overview​

Scientific workflows generate multi-modal data from heterogeneous sources. IOWarp's multi-modality capabilities enable AI agents to reason across:

  • Simulation outputs (petabyte-scale numerical data)
  • Sensor streams (real-time experimental measurements)
  • Literature (scientific publications and citations)
  • System telemetry (performance metrics and logs)

Climate Science​

Combine simulation data (CESM), sensor networks (weather stations), and literature (climate papers) into unified agent context.

Molecular Dynamics​

Integrate LAMMPS trajectories (ADIOS), experimental data (spectroscopy), and theoretical models (computational chemistry papers).

Seismology​

Fuse seismograph data (HDF5), geological surveys (GeoJSON), and research publications (EarthScope database).
