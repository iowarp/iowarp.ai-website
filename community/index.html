Building the Agentic Science Era Together

Join a global community of researchers, developers, and scientists bridging the gap between AI agents and petabyte-scale data.

"We believe that context is the currency of intelligence. In a world drowning in data but starved for meaning, we are building the infrastructure that lets agents reason rather than just retrieve. This isn't just about faster I/O—it's about enabling a new kind of science where agents are first-class citizens."

Why Contribute

Collaborative Innovation

Work alongside climate scientists, AI researchers, systems engineers, and domain experts pushing the boundaries of autonomous discovery.

Real-World Impact

Power agents that predict weather, discover materials, analyze cosmic events, and accelerate scientific breakthroughs across disciplines.

Open Standards

MCP standardizes how agents talk to tools and data. Contribute to the foundation of agentic computing infrastructure.

Cutting-Edge Research

Context engineering, hierarchical memory, agent orchestration—work on the frontier of AI and systems research.
