Real-world applications of context engineering and agentic AI across scientific workflows. IOWarp enables autonomous research through multi-agent systems and optimized workflow orchestration.

Data-Intensive Applications

IOWarp enables autonomous agents to work with petabyte-scale scientific datasets through intelligent context engineering and storage hierarchy management. The platform's HPC I/O optimization capabilities enable efficient processing of data-intensive computing workloads, while workflow orchestration coordinates multi-agent systems across complex scientific workflows.
