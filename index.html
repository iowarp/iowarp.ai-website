What Problems This Solves

Context Assembly Bottleneck

AI agents waste 80% of their time assembling context instead of doing science. IOWarp pre-assembles domain-optimized context, reducing assembly time from minutes to milliseconds.

Learn more â†’

Agent Failures

Without structured access to scientific data formats, agents hallucinate and produce incorrect results. IOWarp provides validated, type-safe interfaces to HDF5, ADIOS, and NetCDF.
