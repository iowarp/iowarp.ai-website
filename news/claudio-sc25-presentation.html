ClaudIO Agentic Data Management Presented at SC25

January 20, 2025 · One min read
Kyle Chard
Co-Principal Investigator

Dr. Kyle Chard presented IOWarp's ClaudIO autonomous agent at SC25 (Supercomputing 2025) in Denver, Colorado.

Presentation Highlights​

  • ClaudIO's autonomous data management capabilities
  • 7.5x performance improvements in seismology workflows
  • Integration with national lab HPC systems
  • Live demonstration of natural language data queries

Resources​

  • Conference website: SC25
  • Presentation slides: Coming soon
  • Related paper: Context Engineering for Autonomous Workflows

Impact​

ClaudIO represents a paradigm shift in how scientists interact with HPC data infrastructure, eliminating the need for manual scripting and enabling natural language driven analysis.
